# Enpal & Flexa Research

## Enpal Overview

- **Founded:** 2017, Berlin, Germany
- **Valuation:** $2.4 billion (2023 funding round)
- **2024 Revenue:** ~$895M
- **Employees:** ~5,000 (majority are local installer teams, 200+ teams)
- **Total Funding:** $655M+ equity; additional $698M line of credit (Nov 2025)
- **Latest Round:** Series D, $110M (Apr 2025), led by TPG

## Enpal Fleet & VPP

- **Connected systems:** 100,000+ private energy systems connected
- **VPP customers:** 40,000+ integrated into VPP (as of Dec 2025)
- **VPP capacity:** 400 MW bundled output
- **VPP participation rate:** ~40% of portfolio (target was 70%)
- **Delay cause:** Grid operators' slow authorization processes for direct marketing — infrastructure installed at customer sites for months but integration blocked by operator authorization delays
- **Customer value:** VPP participants with EVs and storage can earn over EUR 1,000/year through direct marketing vs. feed-in tariffs

## Flexa Joint Venture

- **What:** Joint venture between Enpal (majority stake) and Entrix
- **Mission:** Build Europe's largest virtual power plant
- **CTO:** Sébastien Schikora — previously led Tesla's global stationary battery and Powerwall VPP development
- **Head of Product:** Vjekoslav Salapic — formerly developed VPP solutions at GridX
- **Investment:** EUR 100 million ($107M) to build multi-GW VPP across 80,000+ customers
- **Target:** 1 GW by 2026, multi-GW within a few years
- **Launch:** Trading began October 2024
- **Initial deployment:** 1,000 households, 10 MWh storage, 8 MW solar, ~5 MW battery
- **Market strategy:** Five-minute-interval continuous intraday market, very short-term arbitrage
- **Technology:** Entrix AI-powered electricity trading system + Enpal.One energy management system
- **Revenue streams:** Cost savings for participants, wholesale market trading, grid services (frequency control, voltage stability, capacity reserves)

## Technology Stack (from job postings & articles)

- **Cloud platforms:** GCP, AWS, Azure
- **Container orchestration:** Kubernetes
- **GitOps:** ArgoCD
- **Database:** MongoDB Atlas (time-series "hot storage" for device data)
- **ML/Big Data:** Spark, Dask
- **Hardware:** Enpal.One energy management system (HEMS)
- **AI:** ML models for energy demand forecasting and grid instability prediction

## German Market Context

- **Germany total battery storage (2025):** 24 GWh total installed
- **2025 additions:** 6.57 GWh new capacity
- **Residential share:** 56% of total fleet
- **Home systems installed (2025):** ~366,000 new systems (20% fewer than 2024)
- **Average system size:** 10.3 kWh (up 8%)
- **Competitors:**
  - Sonnen (Shell): 100,000+ batteries in VPP community
  - 1KOMMA5: "Heartbeat" VPP, ~50,000+ installations
  - Next Kraftwerke (Shell): 15,000+ assets, ~10 GW networked capacity
